Des Moines Cedar Falls

I used an alternative document ready function to initialize the map. On mapload, you are seeing my hometown of Webster City and a few markers. When you click the other marker's link for additional info, you will see special information about that marker.

By clicking the link for Des Moines, the map view changes and a marker tooltip is created and opens showing the location of one of my favorite places to walk.

By clicking the Cedar Falls link, the map changes again, and upon clicking the link in the marker you are taken to UNI's webpage.
